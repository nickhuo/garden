## Context

- **Stage focus:** Pre-seed / first institutional check ("first belief check").
- **Why it matters:** Pre-seed is the hardest round to close — these funds are built to back founders the earliest.
- **Tactical note from the source:** The first "yes" moves fast. Have your data room ready *before* you start pitching so you don't lose momentum.
