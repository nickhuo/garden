# Chayenne Zhao

ML-systems researcher and core [[SGLang]] contributor. Posts as **@GenAI_is_real** on X. Moved from **SGLang RL** (training/inference parameter offload-and-refit) to leading **SGLang Omni** (multi-stage model serving) in early 2026.

## Stance

- **"Slice by computation, not modality."** An ML-systems researcher's only goal is to study the computational characteristics of a given computation and design an efficient, robust system for them. Modality is packaging; computation is the real object. This is the throughline of [[Multi-Stage Decoding]].
- **Inference understanding gates RL.** Quotes SGLang RL colleague Junrong: "our RL system optimizations are often bottlenecked by our understanding of the inference system itself" — the reason a RL person went deep on inference.
- Writes candidly about coming to systems from an AI/algorithms background rather than a classic systems track.
