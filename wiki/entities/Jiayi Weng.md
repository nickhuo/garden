# Jiayi Weng

RL researcher and infrastructure engineer (GitHub handle **trinkle23897**). Author of widely-used reinforcement-learning open source: **Tianshou** (PyTorch RL library) and **EnvPool** (`sail-sg/envpool`, high-throughput vectorized RL environments). Background in RL systems and large-scale training infra.

## In this wiki

- Author of [[2026-05 - Weng - Learning Beyond Gradients]], the source that introduces [[Heuristic Learning]] — coding agents iterating on program policies as an alternative to gradient training. His RL-infra background is load-bearing for the argument: the experiments run pure-code policies across Atari/MuJoCo/VizDoom (EnvPool environments) and benchmark them against PPO baselines.
