# Krista Opsahl-Ong

Researcher (Stanford) working on **[[Prompt Optimization]]** for [[Compound AI System|compound AI systems]] in the [[DSPy]] line of work.

- **Lead author** of **[[MIPRO]]** ([[2024-06 - Opsahl-Ong et al - MIPRO Optimizing Instructions and Demonstrations]], EMNLP 2024) — the joint instruction+demonstration optimizer that was SOTA until [[GEPA]].
- **Co-author** of **[[GEPA]]** ([[2026-02 - Agrawal et al - GEPA Reflective Prompt Evolution]]) — the reflective-evolutionary successor that beats her own MIPROv2 by >10%, a clean intra-author progression of the optimizer lineage.

- Frequent collaborators in the wiki orbit: [[Omar Khattab]] (DSPy creator, senior author on both papers), Matei Zaharia, Christopher Potts.
- [[DSPy]] — her optimizers (MIPROv2) ship in the framework.
