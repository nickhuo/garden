# Omar Khattab

Researcher (MIT) working on **[[Compound AI System|compound AI systems]]** and their optimization. Creator of **[[DSPy]]**, the framework that formalizes LLM programs as modular systems with optimizable prompts/weights.

- **Senior author** on **[[MIPRO]]** ([[2024-06 - Opsahl-Ong et al - MIPRO Optimizing Instructions and Demonstrations]], EMNLP 2024) — the joint instruction+demonstration optimizer — and on the **[[GEPA]]** paper ([[2026-02 - Agrawal et al - GEPA Reflective Prompt Evolution]]), which extends the DSPy line of work from MIPRO's few-shot/Bayesian optimization to **reflective evolutionary prompt optimization**.
- Long-running research thread: how should modular LLM systems be *programmed and optimized* rather than hand-prompted — the formalism `Φ = (M, C, X, Y)` that GEPA inherits traces to his DSPy papers (Khattab et al. 2022, 2024).
- Also appears in the wiki via [[2025-10 - Zhang Khattab - Recursive Language Models]] ([[Recursive Language Models]]).
