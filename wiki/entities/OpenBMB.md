# OpenBMB

## What it is

An open-source big-model ecosystem out of the Tsinghua University NLP Lab (THUNLP), associated with ModelBest. Maintains [[ToolBench]]/ToolLLM and the broader "tool learning" research line (Yujia Qin, Zhiyuan Liu, Maosong Sun, et al.), as well as the MiniCPM model family and the BMTools toolkit.

## Why it has a page

Owning org for [[ToolBench]] — the Tsinghua-side counterpart to UC Berkeley's [[Gorilla]] in the open-source tool-use space.
