# Richard Sutton

## Summary

Foundational figure in reinforcement learning and author of the most-cited short argument in modern AI, **The Bitter Lesson** (2019): general methods that scale with computation — search and learning — defeat hand-engineered human knowledge in the long run. He extends this in **The Era of Experience** (2025, with David Silver), arguing that agents should learn from their own streams of experience rather than from human-curated data and hand-built scaffolding.

## Why he matters here

Sutton supplies the deepest theoretical backing for [[Model-Centric Architecture]]: the case that scaffolding the model with brittle hand-written orchestration is a short-term win that scaling will overturn. His work is the counterweight Nick's harness/schema-centric practice must answer.
