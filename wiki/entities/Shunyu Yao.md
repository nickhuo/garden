# Shunyu Yao

Researcher whose work threads through much of the language-agents canon. Co-author on a remarkable run of foundational papers:

- **[[ReAct]]** (2022) — interleaving reasoning and acting.
- **[[Tree of Thoughts]]** (2023) — deliberate search over reasoning paths.
- **[[CoALA]]** (2023, [[2023-09-05 - Sumers et al - Cognitive Architectures for Language Agents]]) — the cognitive-architecture framework for organizing agents.
- **[[tau-bench]]** ([[2024-06-17 - Yao et al - tau-bench]]) — tool-agent reliability benchmark with the [[Pass^k Reliability Metric]].

The arc is coherent: build agent patterns (ReAct/ToT), then a framework to organize them (CoALA), then a benchmark to measure them in production-like settings (τ-bench). A useful entity because so many wiki concepts trace back to this author.
