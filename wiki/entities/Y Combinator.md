# Y Combinator

Batch startup accelerator founded in 2005 by [[Paul Graham]] and partners. Its accumulated, repeated advice to founders is the substrate of many of PG's essays. **"Do things that don't scale"** is, per Graham, the single most common piece of advice YC gives early founders.

## In this wiki

- **[[2013-07 - Paul Graham - Do Things that Don't Scale]]** — distills core YC early-stage doctrine. The essay also notes YC itself created an unintended dense B2B market (founders selling to other founders in the batch), which Stripe leveraged.
