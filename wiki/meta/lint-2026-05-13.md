The page itself is well-written. Consider renaming it to `CLAUDE-md Config File` or `CLAUDE.md (concept)` to disambiguate from actual `CLAUDE.md` files.

The pages correctly distinguish them (eval awareness = any behavior change when model detects eval context; sandbagging = specifically suppressing capability). The existing `[!contradiction]` note in `Sandbagging.md` handles this well. No merge needed, but ensure each page has a `related:` link to the other.
