# TechCrunch — ZeroEntropy Seed Round

Company/funding context (2025-07-09) for [[ZeroEntropy]].

## Facts

- **$4.2M seed** led by **Initialized Capital**; participation from **Y Combinator, Transpose Platform, 22 Ventures, a16z Scout**, plus angels from OpenAI, Hugging Face, and Front. ~$4.4M total raised.
- Co-founders: **Ghita Houir Alami** (CEO) and **Nicholas Pipitone** (CTO).
- Houir Alami: born/raised in Morocco, left at 17 for engineering in France (École Polytechnique), then UC Berkeley master's in mathematics.
- Positioning: an **RAG / retrieval infrastructure** company powering search for AI agents — a **developer tool** (explicitly contrasted with Glean's enterprise-employee search product).
- Core tech: the `ze-rank-1` (zerank-1) reranker, claimed to outperform Cohere and Salesforce on public and private benchmarks.

Funding facts corroborated by the company's own announcement and multiple outlets (high confidence).
