## Promotion policy

A thesis must:
- Be declarative (no "maybe" or "I wonder if")
- Cite ≥2 sources (otherwise it's a draft)
- State the strongest counter-argument as steelman, not strawman
- Specify what evidence would change Nick's mind

If the claim is contested in the literature, also create a `comparisons/<Title>.md` page to file the external positions separately.

## Anti-pattern

Don't write theses as journal entries. They are stake-in-the-ground positions, dated and source-cited. If you're not willing to defend it in print, it's a chat thought, not a thesis.
